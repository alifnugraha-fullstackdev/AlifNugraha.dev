um yeah, this is the first MRT line in Jakarta, and it's pretty decent! maybe don't use it so much, maybe like 6 times a year, but it's a nice experience. the stations are clean and modern, the trains are comfortable and air-conditioned, and the service is generally reliable. the only downside is that it's a bit expensive compared to other public transportation options in Jakarta, but if you can afford it, it's definitely worth trying at least once!

havent tried when it's rush hour yet, but i can imagine that it can get pretty crowded, especially during peak hours. hopefully they can add more rolling stock to accommodate the increasing demand, because it's really a great way to get around the city without having to deal with traffic jams!
