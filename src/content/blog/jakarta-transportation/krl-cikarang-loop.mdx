next up, we have the **Cikarang Loop Line**, i would say this is the most transit-able line in the KRL network, like you've got Tanah Abang, Duri, Manggarai, and the mighty Dukuh Atas station (which is connected to the MRT and LRT).

i'm using this line just to transit from Tanah Abang to Manggarai. or maybe even to Dukuh Atas, but i don't really like to transit to Dukuh Atas because it's always packed with people and the station is not that big. Especially goes to LRT Jabodebek, that's a lot of walk.

but overall, this line is pretty good! it has the new rolling stock, and the frequency is pretty good too. i hope they can maintain the quality of this line.
